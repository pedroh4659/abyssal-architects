  • Chirodectes theomorphus

    A species of jellyfish with tentacles adapted to capture microorganisms. It has internal structures similar to 'chemical gills,' which allow it to use methane and sulfide as alternative energy sources.

  • Germesia amorae

    Soft coral attaches to rocks near hydrothermal vents. Its adaptability, being tolerant to high temperatures and pressures, allows colonies to extend over large areas around the hydrothermal vents.
