The Monera Kingdom showcases a variety of both autotrophic and heterotrophic organisms. Autotrophic and anaerobic organisms are the only producers on the planet, serving as food for other unicellular and multicellular heterotrophs. They also have mutualistic relationships with coral and fish species. The chemosynthesis processes of all species are very similar, with the differentiating factor being the compound used in their chemosynthesis processes, which include hydrogen sulfate, methane, and ammonia. As for heterotrophic bacteria, they are responsible for recycling the organic matter of dead organisms, consuming the remains of other creatures, as well as other bacteria.
